Story Title: Nature Vs pollution, Why?

We decided to do this because we wanted to represent this abrupt natural urgency which is pollution in a cartoon, fictional, action, way as it would be enjoyable and easy to understand for most audiences.

The story:

It starts with the embodiments of nature and pollution and then they eventually fight but pollution has the power to wither away all living things but towards the end nature realizes he has the same power and ends up using it on pollution and reverting all damage caused by it

Our characters: Nature and pollution

Nature is the protagonist and Pollution is the antagonist. Their designs are very nice, Nature is in form of a tree with branches and Pollution is more of like a dark villian design which fits how polution is bad for nature.
